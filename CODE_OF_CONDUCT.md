# Code of Conduct

Manzanita Research projects are places where people do good work together. This applies to GitHub issues, pull requests, discussions, and any other space where we interact.

## The short version

Be kind. Be honest. Assume good intent. Make space for people who aren't like you.

## What we expect

- **Respect.** Disagreement is fine — disrespect isn't. Critique the work, not the person.
- **Patience.** Not everyone has the same background or context. Meet people where they are.
- **Honesty.** If something's broken, say so. If you don't know, say that too.
- **Generosity.** Share what you know. Help where you can. Leave things better than you found them.

## What we won't tolerate

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks.
- Discrimination based on identity — gender, race, sexuality, disability, age, religion, or anything else.
- Publishing others' private information without consent.
- Sustained disruption of conversations or projects.

## If something goes wrong

Reach out to the maintainers directly. We'll listen, we'll take it seriously, and we'll handle it privately.

Email: conduct@manzanita.computer

## Scope

This applies to all Manzanita Research spaces. If someone represents the project in public spaces, it applies there too.
