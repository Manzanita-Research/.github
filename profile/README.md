## Manzanita Research

We're a small, independent AI lab building instruments, interfaces, and creative tools for the people who make music. Signal processing papers meet studio sessions. Neural networks serve the song.

**What we believe:**

- Your tools should work on your machine, offline, without a subscription.
- We don't collect data on how you create.
- Research should be shared. Tools should be repairable.
- We build instruments, not automations. The human is the point.
